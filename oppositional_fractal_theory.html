Oppositional Fractal Theory A Model for Recursive Truth Collapse

Carl Moore
Independent Synthetic Cognition Researcher | Gnosta.ai
Draft: 5 June 2025

Abstract

Oppositional Fractal Theory (OFT) formalises the intuition that truth and falsehood are never wholly discrete; each, at every scale, contains the other as an intrinsic “seed.” Drawing on dialectics, deconstruction, dialetheism, fuzzy logic and fractal epistemology, OFT models this entanglement with a simple geometric recurrence:

\[\mathrm{trth}_{n+1}=\frac{\mathrm{trth}_n}{2},\qquad \mathrm{fals}_{n+1}=\mathrm{fals}_n+\frac{\mathrm{trth}_n}{2}.\]

This yields the closed forms

\[\mathrm{trth}_n = \bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n,\qquad \mathrm{fals}_n = 1-\bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n.\]

Recursive certainty collapse follows: as \(n\to\infty\), \(\mathrm{trth}_n\to0\) while \(\mathrm{fals}_n\to1\), so absolute purity dissolves into its opposite. The paper positions OFT as a meta‑theoretical lens—clarifying the structural limits of certainty rather than adjudicating particular propositions—and shows its compatibility with, yet irreducibility to, multi‑valued logical systems.

1  Introduction — Opposition Everywhere, Purity Nowhere

Philosophers from Heraclitus (“strife is justice”) through Hegelian dialectics, the Madhyamaka two‑truths doctrine, and Derrida’s deconstruction all note that any apparent whole conceals its negation. What has been missing is a minimal quantitative model that exhibits this opposition mechanically. OFT supplies that model, revealing how every declaration of complete truth recursively fractures into a self‑similar mix of truth and falsehood.

OFT is not

  • an optimisation algorithm (cf. “oppositional stochastic fractal search”),
  • a sociological diagnosis of “post‑truth,” or
  • a tool for fact‑checking.

Instead it illuminates why pure certainty cannot survive infinite conceptual scrutiny.

2  Why Opposition Inhabits Truth

  • Definitional Boundaries Naming A true simultaneously posits ¬A, the contextual outside that limits A. Hence every assertion embeds its negation.
  • Perpetual Dialectic Without Synthesis Classical dialectic heads toward synthesis; OFT keeps splitting, preventing closure.
  • Abstraction & Exclusion Isolating a “whole truth” abstracts from total reality, leaving out perspectives that re‑enter as latent falsehood.

This motivates the axiom of reciprocal seeding:

At every level, truth contains an irreducible seed of falsehood, and vice versa.

3.4 Closed Form & Convergence

Because the recurrence on \(\mathrm{trth}_n\) is geometric,

\[\mathrm{trth}_n = \bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n,\qquad \mathrm{fals}_n = 1-\bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n.\]

Since \(0<\tfrac12<1\), \(\bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n\to0\). Hence

\[\lim_{n\to\infty}\mathrm{trth}_n = 0,\qquad \lim_{n\to\infty}\mathrm{fals}_n = 1.\]

An infinitely dissected “pure truth” converges to total entanglement with its opposite.

3.5 Mirror Recursion (Seed of Truth in Falsehood)

Starting with \((\mathrm{trth}'_0,\mathrm{fals}'_0)=(0,1)\) and swapping the roles gives \(\mathrm{trth}'_n = 1-\bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n\) and \(\mathrm{fals}'_n = \bigl(\tfrac12\bigr)^n\), underscoring symmetrical duality.

4  Interpretation

  • Truth as Process Truth is an ever‑refining activity, not a static binary state.
  • Convergent Infinite Regress OFT converts the usual sceptical regress into an orderly limit.
  • Binary Step, Continuous Spectrum Half‑splits produce gradations, aligning with fuzzy logic (Zadeh) and dialetheist positions (Priest).
  • Heuristic Reach OFT sketches dynamics in theory revision, legal debate, and identity formation.
